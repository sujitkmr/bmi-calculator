    BMI Ranges (Adults)

    • Underweight: < 18.5
    • Normal weight: 18.5 – 24.9
    • Overweight: 25 – 29.9
    • Obesity: ≥ 30

    Note: BMI is a general indicator and doesn’t account for muscle mass, age, or body composition. Always seek professional medical advice for personalized guidance.
