About us

Effective Date: August 9, 2025

Welcome to gymbmi.com. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect the data you provide when using our website.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personally identifiable information unless you voluntarily provide it. However, we may automatically collect non-personal data such as browser type, operating system, referral source, and IP address for statistical and analytical purposes.

2. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies are small data files stored on your device. You can disable cookies through your browser settings, but it may affect website functionality.

3. Google AdSense and Third-Party Ads

This website uses Google AdSense to serve ads. Google may use cookies (including the DART cookie) to show ads based on your visits to this and other websites. You can opt out of personalized ads by visiting the Google Ads Settings or read more at Google’s Advertising Policy.

4. How We Use Your Information

If you contact us via forms or email, we will only use your name, email, or phone number to respond to your query. We do not sell, rent, or share this information with third parties.

5. Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

6. Data Security

We implement standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. All changes will be reflected on this page with a revised effective date. Continued use of the website indicates your acceptance of the changes.
